Polka Dot

One well rooted cutting, with leaf or leaves.

An amazing decorative plant that looks great in any home or workplace.

Easy first plant, just water when leaves are drooping and looking sad, feed every couple of months.

Will be ready to pot in soil as soon as you receive it.

Pot not included.

Care Tips:


  • Bright indirect sunlight

  • water when soil surface is dry to touch

  • any humidity, but a humid environment will lead to bigger leaves

  • Adding extra drainage to the potting mix will help prevent root rot (orchid bark or pumice stone works well).

Watermelon peperomia (Peperomia argyreia)

One unrooted leaf cutting taken from a Watermelon peperomia (Peperomia argyreia) houseplant.

These beautiful bushy plants are easy to grow and look after, they look great on a bookshelf or on a kitchen window, perfect as a single plant in a small apartment or as part of a group of plants on display.

flowers a funky looking green stem, prefers to be dry.

Plant will have one large leaf with smaller leaves around the bottom amongst the roots.

Care Tips:

  • peperomia requires bright indirect light. Avoid direct sunlight as this can cause the variegation on its leaves to fade.

  • plant prefers increased humidity but will tolerate normal humidity levels. Avoid dry air. leaves will droop and curl when thirsty.

  • Keep soil lightly moist by watering when the top 1-2 inches of potting mix has dried out.

  • Adding extra drainage to the potting mix will help prevent root rot (orchid bark or pumice stone works well).
