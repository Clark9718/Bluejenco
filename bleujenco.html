About BleujenCo

BleujenCo started off on Esty selling propagated houseplants by Scott in Cornwall. Scott started this small business' after gaining too many plants in his flat. Starting with only four listings of some basic houseplants, then growing his store have have 11 plants in total, with aspirations of expanding to potted plants, soil and pots.

He gained a passion for plants, particularly growing them from seed, during the covid-19 pandemic (like a lot of people) and after a year or so of propagating and seed sowing, he ended up acquiring a few too many, running out of places to put them. After having a baby, and having to put plants in higher places to get them off the floor, he came to the conclusion that he needed to get rid of some as they were just getting in the way. He gave a few away to friends and family and they were happy to see the plants grow and thrive with very little work. So after a little thought an planning he started to open up and Esty store.
